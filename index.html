The Goals

We have a few simple goals for our application (might be planned or unfinished):

Intuitive

We needed our app to be simple and enjoyable to use, with no hard to use features

Statistically Accurate

Accuracy and reliability was at the forefront of our algorithm, making sure our statistics and recommendations are perfect

Easy Access

We wanted the information to be stored safely, without the need for you to worry about it taking up space on your device

Clear Graphics

Bright, easy to read diagrams for all of our application is a must to ensure you are able to understand the recommendations and data

Realistic Recommendations

Goals are hard to achieve if they are far fetched; hence our algorithms are realistic and suited for your needs

Happiness Index

We wanted a clear and rewarding system for you to see how happy you truly are, with ways to reward you for becoming happier
